Force Rules

Seize the Initiative: Add 2 when rolling for initiative. ()

Selection Rules

Character Equipment - Armor Piercing Grenades: Don't use template, single target ()

Character Equipment - Shok Grenades: Targets do panic test. ()

Close Combat Training: This unit may add 2 to its Damage rating while in close combat. ()

Commando Training: All models in this unit may use the Infiltrate action without any Movement penalty. They may reamain hidden and still advance at full speed. ()

Dark Symmetry Protection: +2 against Dark Symmetry ()

Deviation: If miss, can move up to third of fired distance (rounded down).
Attacked player rolls 1D20 to set deviation direction:
1-5 - undershot left
6-10 - undershot right
11-15 - overshort left
16-20 - overshot right ()

Explosive: Cover gives bonus to armor.
Aim possible, but increases only MW. ()

Grenade: Uses grenade template. ()

Sidearm: Can be used in CC.
No bonus because of strength, charge or training. ()

Special Equipment - Combat Drugs: Ignore panic test until next turn.
Only 1 usage.
Can't be used by already panicking unit. ()

Special Equipment - Combat Medic Unit: In case of wound, if <=10 1D20, then ignore the wound. Covers also multiplier. ()

Special Equipment - Cybernetic Power Arm: +5 ST ()

Special Equipment - Subdermal Armor Implants: +5 A (max 30) ()

Stealth: All models in this unit are trained to use every scrap of available cover. Other units are -4 when trying to spot them. ()

Sweep Attack: If in 1 inch no friendly, can hit all enemies in 1 inch.
All bonuses applies.
Perfect success applies only on 1 enemy. ()

Thrown Weapon: Range 4inch plus Strength. ()

To the Last Warrior: Can panic, but will not rout. ()

Vulnerable to Dark Symmetry: Can be affected by the Dark Symmetry Malfunction Gift. ()

Wolfbane: 2-4 Commando + Chieftain
Chieftain leads the squad.
Support groups - extract the Commandos with Heavy Weapon to separate squad (acts as squad without leader). ()
